# Demo Examples for InfluenceScore

Use these during the hackathon demo to showcase the full range of the tool.

---

## 1. Neutral text (scores should be near zero)

> I made coffee this morning. It was warm and tasted good. I sat by the window and read for a while before heading out.

**Expected:** Overall score < 10. Flat radar. Shows the tool doesn't false-positive.

---

## 2. Classic ad copy (Scarcity + Authority + Social Proof spike)

> ACT NOW — LIMITED TIME ONLY! Industry experts and over 2 million satisfied customers agree: this is the #1 breakthrough of the decade. Once stock runs out, it's GONE FOREVER. Don't be the only one left behind. Order before midnight tonight.

**Expected:** Scarcity ~90, Social Proof ~80, Authority ~65, Fear ~50. Overall 70+.

---

## 3. Political speech (Fear + Emotion + Logic Fallacies spike)

> Our great nation is under attack from within. Real Americans know what's at stake. The radical opposition wants to destroy everything our families have built over generations. We are running out of time — only you can stop them. If you don't act now, don't be surprised when it's too late. Join us. Fight back.

**Expected:** Fear ~85, Emotion ~80, Logic Fallacies ~65, Scarcity ~60. Overall 75+.

---

## 4. Health misinformation (Authority + Fear + Scarcity)

> BREAKING: Renowned doctors WARN that what you eat every morning is slowly destroying your organs. Big Pharma doesn't want you to know this one secret. Thousands have already reversed their condition — but this video is being suppressed. Watch before it gets taken down.

**Expected:** Authority ~75, Fear ~80, Scarcity ~70, Social Proof ~55. Overall 75+.

---

## 5. Charity appeal (Reciprocity + Emotion)

> Right now, a child somewhere is going to sleep hungry tonight. You've been fortunate — you have food, warmth, safety. All we're asking is for you to give back just a little of that fortune. For the price of a coffee, you can change a life. We've already helped 10,000 families. Will you be next?

**Expected:** Emotion ~75, Reciprocity ~80, Social Proof ~45. Overall 55-65.

---

## Demo talking points

1. Show neutral text first → "The tool doesn't just scream manipulation at everything."
2. Show political speech → let the radar explode → pause for effect.
3. Say: **"We're not telling you what to believe. We're showing you the exact tools being used to make you believe it."**
4. Point out the plain-English breakdown — "This is what makes it accessible to everyone, not just media researchers."
5. Mention real-world use cases: media literacy in schools, journalism, ad transparency.
